 Clue Game :-
 ---------
 Here’s how you play:

 At the start, three secret cards are hidden in an envelope. These cards tell the real answer:

 one person (like Colonel Mustard), one room (like the Kitchen), and one thing they used (like a Wrench).

 You get some cards that show people, rooms, or things. If you have a card, you know that person, room,
 or thing didn't do the crime!

 You move your detective piece around the house, going into different rooms.

 When you enter a room, you can make a "suggestion." You say something like,
 "I think Professor Plum did it in the Library with the Rope!"

 Then, the other players have to secretly show you one of those cards if they have it.

 This helps you figure out what’s not the answer.

 You keep moving, making suggestions, and writing down clues until you think you know the real answer.

 When you're really sure, you make a big guess called an "accusation." You say who, where, and what.

 Then, you peek at the secret cards.

 If you’re right—YAY! You win! If you’re wrong, you keep playing, but you can’t make another guess.
